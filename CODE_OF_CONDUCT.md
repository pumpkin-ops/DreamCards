# Code of Conduct

DreamCards contributors are expected to maintain a respectful, inclusive, and technically constructive environment.

## Expected behavior

- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Explain tradeoffs and provide reproducible evidence.
- Respect differences in language, experience, identity, and communication style.
- Treat moderation, safety, privacy, and accessibility concerns as first-class engineering input.
- Obtain permission before sharing another person's content or private communication.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or sexualized conduct.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Knowingly submitting infringing, abusive, or unsafe image content.
- Manipulating issue discussions, reviews, or community votes.
- Using AI-generated comments to overwhelm maintainers or avoid personal accountability.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `2819092941@qq.com`. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close contributions, issue warnings, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

Enforcement decisions should be documented privately, applied consistently, and protect reporters from retaliation.
